I am opheliadorothy, a creative artist whose practice flows freely across boundaries. My work weaves together music composition and performance, fashion styling and design, photography, programming, and the electric energy of DJing. Each discipline informs the others, forming a personal language that explores beauty, emotion, rhythm, and innovation.

At present, I am wholeheartedly pursuing the path I have long envisioned. My immediate horizon centers on the release of my debut album—a deeply personal collection that distills years of sonic exploration, visual storytelling, and lived experience.

From there, I am steadily building toward the next chapter: the establishment of Fleur Fam, my own music company dedicated to nurturing bold, boundary-pushing talent and creating space for artistry that feels both timeless and fearlessly forward-looking.

Whether through a carefully composed melody, a captured moment in light, an unexpected fusion of code and sound, or the curation of an unforgettable set, I seek to invite connection, evoke feeling, and celebrate the quiet power of creation.

Welcome to my world.
I’m just getting started.
